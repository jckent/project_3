Why we do this

Some Facts

Finding mental health care is hard. Even when you have health insurance. In one study, researchers attempting to schedule an appointment were only able to do so 26% of the time after making 720 calls. As anyone who has tried before knows, going down a list from your insurance provider can be frustrating, at best. The information provided can be completely out of date. The therapist may no longer be accepting new patients, or your insurance, or may just never call you back. The number listed could go to a McDonald's. It's no wonder that by the fourth call, you feel even worse.

To the Rescue

Extra Spoons tries to save you some of that energy by providing you accurate, up-to-date information on mental health professionals. The information here is provided directly by the providers themselves and is updated every 90 days, ensuring that you won't end up calling a jewelry store when you are in crisis. Save your spoons for other things in your life.
